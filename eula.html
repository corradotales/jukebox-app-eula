New Definitions

  • Credits: Virtual currency tied to your account, used exclusively to generate QR Codes in the App. Credits do not expire and are non-transferable.
  • Digital Service: Credits and generated QR Codes are considered immediately delivered digital services under Art. 59 of the Italian Consumer Code, thus not eligible for withdrawal rights.
  • JukeShorts: The short-form video feature within the App allowing users to upload, share, and discover video content. Videos are limited to 60 seconds duration and 700MB file size, with automatic optimization applied during upload.
  • QR Code Session: A time-limited session created for event management, including a unique QR code that can be activated for 24-hour periods. Sessions include configuration data (language, playlist, images, social links).
    • Verified Account: An account that has successfully completed the email verification process, granting access to all platform features including QR code activation and song request management.
    • Unverified Account: An account that has not completed or is pending email verification, with restricted access to certain features.
    • Verification Code: A temporary 8-character alphanumeric code sent via email to confirm account ownership and enable full platform access.
    • Verification Session: A time-limited (10-minute) process during which a verification code is valid for account confirmation.
  • PDF Generation: The process of creating printable documents containing QR codes and event information. Available in two formats: Complete PDF (with instructions) or QR-only PDF (minimal format).
  • Tips & Donations: A feature allowing users to send and receive monetary tips through the platform. Tips are processed via Stripe Connect with automatic deduction of a 4% platform commission and applicable Stripe processing fees.
  • Stripe Connect Account: A connected Stripe Express account that enables users to receive payouts from tips. Requires completion of Stripe's onboarding process including identity verification.
  • Platform Commission: A 4% fee deducted from each tip received, which constitutes JukeBox App's revenue for providing the tipping infrastructure.
  • Direct Charge: A Stripe Connect feature applied to tips under €5 where the donor pays all Stripe processing fees in addition to the tip amount.
  • Payout Schedule: The timing of fund transfers from Stripe to the recipient's bank account, typically following a 7-day rolling schedule for Italian accounts as determined by Stripe.

    6.1 QR Code Creation and Usage

    6.2 PDF Generation Features

    The App provides two types of printable PDF documents:

    • Complete PDF: Contains QR code, event details, instructions, and JukeBox branding. Suitable for professional events and formal distribution.
    • QR-only PDF: Contains only the QR code in your selected size:
      • Small (60% page): For stickers and small placements
      • Medium (80% page): For flyers and standard printing
      • Large (95% page): For posters and large format printing

    PDF documents are:

    • Generated locally in your browser and downloaded to your device
    • Not stored on our servers or cloud storage
    • Available for immediate download after QR code creation

    6.3 Session Management

    QR code sessions:

    • Are automatically purged from our systems 30 days after expiration
